Tokenization turns text into numbers. Embeddings turn sparse IDs into vectors.
Language models use tokens, embeddings, residual streams, logits, and sampling.

Byte-level BPE begins with UTF-8 bytes, then learns frequent adjacent merges.
The tokenizer should preserve spaces, punctuation, emoji like 🔥, and accents like café.
